Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Fliptern

When will Fliptern launch?

We're currently in development and plan to launch in early 2025. Join our waitlist to be the first to know when we go live!

How is Fliptern different from other job platforms?

Fliptern uses a swipe-based matching system specifically designed for internships, eliminating ghosting and providing instant feedback to both students and employers.

Is Fliptern free for students?

Yes! Fliptern will be completely free for students to use. We believe in making internship opportunities accessible to everyone.

How can employers get involved?

Employers can partner with us to post internship opportunities and access our curated pool of talented students. Contact us to learn more about our employer partnerships.
